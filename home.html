About Us

BOOK'D is a community-driven platform dedicated to making reading more accessible and affordable for everyone. We believe that books should have multiple lives and be shared among readers.

Founded in 2025, our mission is to create a sustainable ecosystem for book lovers where pre-loved books find new homes instead of ending up in landfills. We've helped thousands of readers find their next favorite book at a fraction of the retail price.

Our platform makes it easy to buy and sell used books in excellent condition. We carefully verify all listings to ensure quality and authenticity, providing a seamless experience for both buyers and sellers.
